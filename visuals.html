🔭 [ TrES-5 b ]

A giant planet that orbits a star so faint and dim that human telescopes had to work extra hard to spot it.

🔭 [ Qatar-2 b ]
Distance: Far (~590 ly)
Weight: Massive (2.5x Jupiter)
Year Length: Only 1.3 Days!
This planet is stuck and cannot spin. One side permanently faces its sun and roasts, while the other side faces frozen dark space.
🎨 Visual Choice: Matte black. The heavy atmosphere behaves like a giant sponge that completely swallows up all incoming starlight.
🔭 [ WASP-10 b ]
Distance: Far (~460 ly)
Weight: Huge (Over 3x Jupiter)
Year Length: Only 3.1 Days!
An incredibly tightly packed planet. It travels along an egg-shaped oval path and is hard and dense like our Moon.
🎨 Visual Choice: Dark navy. The immense weight of the planet squeezes its air into thick, glass-like sapphire clouds.
🔭 [ WASP-49 b ]
Distance: Far (~635 ly)
Weight: Light (1/3 of Jupiter)
Year Length: Only 2.8 Days!
A lighter planet trapped inside a fast gas cloud. Scientists think a hidden, highly volcanic moon is blasting out this gas.
🎨 Visual Choice: Ash gray. High amounts of salt vapor eat away all warm orange and yellow colors, leaving a ghostly silhouette.
🔭 [ WASP-52 b ]
Distance: Far (~570 ly)
Weight: Medium (Half of Jupiter)
Year Length: Only 1.7 Days!
A puffy gas planet that sits too close to its sun. The solar heat is boiling the air so fast that it blows off into space.
🎨 Visual Choice: Icy blue rim. Highlights a massive tail of escaping gas that the planet leaves behind like a comet.
🔭 [ WASP-140 b ]
Distance: Medium (~380 ly)
Weight: Massive (2.5x Jupiter)
Year Length: Only 2.2 Days!
A heavy world skimming across a narrow edge around a very young star that is spinning at chaotic speeds.
🎨 Visual Choice: Violet rings. Represents the constant bombardment of intense, high-energy UV rays hitting the upper gas layers.
