OPERATIONAL PIPELINE SUMMARY: We utilize Google Colab to run the EXOTIC pipeline. By plugging in raw astronomical data, we generate light-curves—visual measurements of a star's brightness over time. When a planet passes in front of its star, it blocks a tiny amount of light, creating a 'dip' in the curve. These dips allow researchers to calculate the planet's orbital period (year length) and atmospheric temperature.
