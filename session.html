// An Immersive Poetry Installation • CT Tech Week

Installation Brief Date: Tuesday, June 2nd
Host: Angela N. Moore, PI
Location: The Ely Center of Contemporary Art
11:00 - 12:00

Gallery Welcome & Gathering

Doors open at the gallery space. Meet the host, settle into the space, and pick up your interactive event packets and materials.

Early Arrival Art Materials
12:00 - 12:15

Introduction & Creative Overview

The session formally begins. A short introduction outlining how to conduct citizen science research for NASA, and how we map those deep space discoveries into creative writing and contemporary poetry.

Opening Space Data
12:15 - 12:35

Planet Exploration & Word Selection

Explore our cosmic planetary guides using the digital Emaze Hub or physical display sheets. Find a world that speaks to you, and choose a single, powerful word that describes its atmosphere.

Interactive QR Portal Planet Guides
12:35 - 12:55

Invisible Ink Lab & Interactive UV Craft

Write your chosen planet and your selected inspiration word on your paper slate using specialized invisible ink markers. Step under the blacklights to watch your hidden words instantly glow into view.

UV Light Invisible Ink Word Selection
12:55 - 1:00

Gallery Collection & Wrap Up

Finished word cards are collected. After the event, the host will expand your chosen words into custom archive poems to be featured in our upcoming post-event digital showcase.

Creative Archive Closing
